Rise of the Tomb Raider

I played this game via Xbox Game Pass, and it's one of the best linear story based games present on the service. It's the second installment in the rebooted survivor trilogy of the tomb raider games.
The story continues after the events of the first game and we follow Lara Croft on an adventure where she discovers many tombs and historical ruins.
Here are some of the featurs of this game:

  • Mainly linear action-adventure game with some side missions of tomb exploration.
  • A charismatic and tough protagonist.
  • Great mechanics for the time and a crisp story.
  • Graphically intense but well optimized.

It is my favourite linear story game of all time as the game provides a way to make exploring the tombs fun while still keeping a tight storyline.
