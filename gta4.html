Grand Theft Auto IV

I replayed this gem of a game again after about 15 years, when I played it first as a kid. And I must say this game still holds up. Arguably, it has the best story in any GTA game.
The story follows the life of Niko Bellic, who comes to Liberty City in search of a new life. Niko is a former armyman and the horrors of war haunts him throughout the game.
What makes this game more unique from other GTA titles is that this game follows a more dark and grim story of a criminal. Here are some of the features of this game:

  • It's an open-world action-adventure game set in the 2000's.
  • Features very realistic driving mechanics absent from other GTA titles.
  • A very unique friends system, with activities around town.
  • Perfect balance of goofy GTA elements with an emotional story at its core.

I just love the Grand Theft Auto series as a whole but GTA 4 holds a special place in my heart. Niko Bellic, a flawed but charming protagonist with other supporting characters makes this game one of my favourite of all time.
