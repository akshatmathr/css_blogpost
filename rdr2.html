Red Dead Redemption 2

I recently completed the main storyline of Red Dead Redemption 2(rdr2). And I must say this game one of the greatest story in video game history.
The story follows the life of Arthur Morgan who is a part of the Van Der Linde gang headed by Dutch and Hosea. This game is a prequel to Red Dead Redemption(2010) and follows a different protagonist. This game's story is very grounded in nature and very tragic.
Here are some of the features of the game:

  • Mainly a single player action-adventure game with an online mode too.
  • Best graphics of any modern game.
  • Unique mechanics such a "Dead Eye" makes the game accessible to casual gamers too.
  • One of the biggest map for an open-world game with numerous points of interest.

Overall, Red Dead Redemption 2 is my favourite game of all time as it has the most memorable and fleshed out characters in any video game that I've played with well optimized gameplay and top of the line graphics.
